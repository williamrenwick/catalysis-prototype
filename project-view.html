Raising High The Union Flag

The
Challenge

HERE is a nascent brand in the UK with some early champions in the media space but still in the beginning phases of building wider mainstream brand recognition. HERE tasked Catalysis with supporting their first campaign to talk to UK consumers, an online competition leveraging social media, which they knew they wanted to focus around their fleet of HERE field cars â€” road vehicles decked out with state of the art 3D scanning technology that drive worldwide gathering up to the minute data that lets HERE produce detailed, comprehensive digital maps.

The
Strategy

We built a microsite for the #spotHERE competition that asked users to drag and drop a HERE field car icon onto the location they believed the car to be in at the time, based on a series of clues which were tweeted daily from the @here Twitter account. The contest was promoted across channels including Facebook, Outbrain and Unruly. We created a range of creative assets including a 3D London flyover animated video.

The
Results

Our digital and content teams delivered the campaign microsite, plus video, digital ad creative and social media assets in the exacting turnaround time of six weeks from brief to go-live.
